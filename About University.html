      Official Information & Academic Departments

      About UET Mardan

      The University of Engineering & Technology (UET) Mardan is a public sector engineering university located in Mardan, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan. It was established in 2018 after being upgraded from a campus of UET Peshawar. The university is recognized by the Higher Education Commission (HEC) and the Pakistan Engineering Council (PEC).

      Academic Departments

      • Department of Electrical Engineering
        Focuses on power systems, electronics, control systems, and communication technologies.
      • Department of Telecommunication Engineering
        Deals with signal processing, wireless communication, networking, and modern telecom systems.
      • Department of Computer Software Engineering
        Covers software development, programming, mobile applications, and software quality assurance.
      • Department of Computer Science
        Offers education in artificial intelligence, databases, cybersecurity, algorithms, and systems.
      • Department of Civil Engineering
        Focuses on structures, transportation, geotechnical engineering, and construction management.
      • Department of Mechanical Engineering
        Covers mechanics, thermodynamics, manufacturing processes, and machine design.
      • Department of Natural Sciences & Humanities
        Provides fundamental courses such as Mathematics, Physics, English, and Humanities.

      Special Centers

      Center of Artificial Intelligence
      The center focuses on research, innovation, and training in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Data Science to support modern technological development.

      Programs Offered

      • Undergraduate Programs (BS/BSc Engineering)
      • Postgraduate Programs (MS/MSc)
      • Doctoral Programs (PhD)
